⌘R Hack Day

Where you do the things you love doing, better.

October 24, 2014
Lyst Studios, London
With from NSLondon

The Hack Day is the third and final day of ⌘R Conf. It's a chance to apply some of the ideas that are bound to be fighting to get out by the end of the conference. You'll also get to work with some of the awesome people you'll meet during the conference.

There will be a couple of fairly structured workshops as well as some more freeform hacking challenges.

Venue

The ⌘R Conf Hack Day will be held in Lyst Studios, 48 Hoxton Square, N1 6NN. It’s yet another fantastic venue in the same brilliant location as the main conference.
